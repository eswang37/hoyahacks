What is HoyaHacks?

36 Hours to Build Something Brilliant

HoyaHacks is Georgetown University's premier annual hackathon — a 36-hour sprint where 300+ students from across the country come together to learn, build, and ship projects that matter.

Whether you're a seasoned engineer or writing your very first line of code, HoyaHacks is your stage. We welcome all skill levels, all backgrounds, and every wild idea you walk through the door with.

Expect free food, swag, $20K+ worth of prizes, and a whole lot of energy on the Hilltop.
